How we handle your data

What data we collect

We collect different categories of data throughout your journey with us. For example, when you sign up, we ask for basic details like your username and password so you can log in safely. While you browse, we remember your preferences to help you find things you love. For deliveries, we need to know the delivery company you choose and the delivery address to ensure your orders arrive where and when they should.
